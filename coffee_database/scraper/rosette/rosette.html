Koji Series

Rossette Coffee Lab introduces the Koji Series, featuring two Koji-processed naturals: a robusta from Kerehaklu Estate and an arabica from Ratnagiri Estate. Koji (Aspergillus oryzae), a fungus traditionally used to ferment rice in Japan, is applied to coffee cherries to create unique flavor nuances. This innovative process unlocks distinctive tastes that go beyond traditional yeast or bacterial fermentations, offering an exceptional coffee experience.
